# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the DeltaDotta community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing
empathy, welcoming differing viewpoints, giving and accepting constructive
feedback, and taking responsibility for mistakes. Unacceptable behavior includes
harassment, discriminatory jokes or language, publishing private information,
and other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards
and may remove comments, commits, code, issues, or contributions that do not
align with them. Report abusive behavior privately through the repository’s
maintainer contact or GitHub’s reporting tools; do not post sensitive reports in
public issues.
