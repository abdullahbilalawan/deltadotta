# DeltaDotta governance

DeltaDotta is maintainer-led and open to contributions.

## Decisions

Maintainers make release, security, roadmap, and package-contract decisions in
public issues or pull requests whenever possible. For significant changes, a
proposal should state the user problem, compatibility impact, safety impact, and
test plan. Maintainers aim for consensus and make the final call when consensus
is not practical.

## Maintainers and releases

Active maintainers review contributions, enforce the Code of Conduct, and may
delegate ownership of templates or provider adapters. Releases require a passing
CI build and a review of user-facing changes. Security reports are handled
privately under [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Becoming a maintainer

Consistent constructive contributions, sound judgment around portability and
safety, and respectful collaboration are the path to a maintainer invitation.
